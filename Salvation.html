What is Salvation?

Salvation, is a term that is primarily identified in religious beliefs as a term of being saved or protected from harm. In Adventism and Islam, salvation is a primary part of their individual religious beliefs. Through this assessment, I will be discussing differences, similarities and the significance of salvation in terms of a political, social, and personal view according to the two religious beliefs. So what is the deeper meaning behind salvation and why is it important in these two faiths? Let us explore salvation through these two different views.
